# Brand Overview

## Brand Name

OnePage

## Tagline

**Build your online identity.**

Alternative:

**Your story. One beautiful page.**

---

# Brand Mission

Enable anyone to build a beautiful personal website without writing code.

OnePage removes technical barriers and lets creators focus on presenting themselves professionally.

---

# Brand Vision

Become the easiest and most elegant platform for creating a personal website.

Not just another Link in Bio tool.

A place where people express who they are.

# Design Principles

Less is more.

Whitespace is part of the design.

Motion supports usability.

Color has meaning.

Consistency is more important than creativity.

Every component should feel like it belongs to the same product.

When in doubt, remove instead of adding.
